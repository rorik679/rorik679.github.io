Part 4: Release & Critics

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom released on the 12th of May 2023 for â‚¬69.99, exclusively on Nintendo Switch.

Before release though, between the the 2nd and 3rd of May 2023, datamined ROMs of the game leaked online, and people were able to successfully emulate the game on a PC. This leak was one of the most disastrous that's ever happened to a game pre-release, as people were able to not only obtain the game for free and play it on illegal hardware, but they were completing it early and spoiling people who were planning on playing the game legally at launch. Those who were looking forward to release had to avoid spoilers being spread everywhere online.

Nintendo started cracking down harshly on anyone spreading these illegal copies, and started a hunt to try find the person responsible. There are no reports of the person ever being found.

Regardless, the game's release schedule continued, and between 2-3 days before the game released Nintendo lifted the review embargo on journalists.

Tears of the Kingdom opened to glowing 10/10 reviews across the board. Below you'll find some of the biggest gaming journalism networks and their reviews on the game.

"A remarkable sequel that somehow raises the bar even higher into the clouds." - Tom Marks, IGN

"This is The Legend of Zelda at its finest, borrowing the best pieces and qualities from across the franchise's history and creating something new that is emotionally resonant, captivating, and endlessly rewarding." Steve Watts, GameSpot

"A glorious, triumphant sequel to one of the best video games of all time." Alana Hagues, Nintendo Life

Asking the gaming community to get along is usually a Sisyphean task, but in the weeks following Tears of the Kingdom's release practically everyone was just simply enjoying the game and talking about it together. This time is always my favourite example of how amazing video games can be for bringing people together no matter who they are, and sharing one singular thing that they all have a unified love for.

The Legend of Zelda had once again proved why it's widely considered the greatest video game series in history.
